Maya Moss

Short Bio

Naya Moss is known for her work in cybersecurity and her STEM career. She is also the founder of a platform called Fravius. Fravius is a platform that serves as a safe space for black women and helps in their communities and using mentorship to assist them. Not only did she founded Fravius but she also founded IT andCreative Studio,and Namos Lab.She is also the Head of Diversify Tech Europe.

Important Facts
She has worked in IT and cyber security for 13 years.
Her career started when she was a teenager. (she would do odd jobs such as building desktops, freelance helpdesk, setting up home networks, and web development.)
At one point in her teens she worked as a Junior System Administrator on Wall Street.
She also worked as a Cloud Infrastructure Engineer.
